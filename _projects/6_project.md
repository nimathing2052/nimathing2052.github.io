Research infrastructure built during a short-term expert engagement with the Weizenbaum Institute's *Platform Algorithms and Digital Propaganda* group and the University of Bern (December 2025).

The framework supports longitudinal algorithmic auditing of Yandex and Google in the context of Kremlin-disinformation research. It is built on Botasaurus with WebGL/Canvas fingerprint evasion, proxy support, parallelization, CAPTCHA handling, and structured logging. The guiding principle was research infrastructure first, code second: the system is reproducible, modular, and documented for hand-off to social-science researchers.

**Stack:** Python, Botasaurus, proxy orchestration, structured logging.
**Code:** [github.com/nimathing2052/webscraper-botasaurus](https://github.com/nimathing2052/webscraper-botasaurus) (this is also private, reach out to me individually if you'd want to know more)
