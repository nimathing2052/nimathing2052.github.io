A volunteer research contribution to [DeepTrace](https://deepfake-research.org/), a project on deepfake detection in South Asian contexts (November 2025 – January 2026).

I worked togther with Team establish annotation guidelines and benchmarks for AI-generated visual content and deepfake detection, and contributed to the design of the dataset-collection interface. The work addresses a persistent gap in detection research: most benchmarks under-represent South Asian faces, languages, and visual contexts.

**Role:** Research assistant — annotation protocol, benchmarking, dataset UI/UX.
**Project:** [South Asia Deepfake Detection](https://sites.google.com/view/south-asia-deepfake-detection/team)
**Research Mentor:**: [Dilrukshi Gamage]https://www.dilrukshigamage.org/)
